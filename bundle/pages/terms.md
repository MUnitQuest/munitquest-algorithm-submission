## 👥 3. Team Participation Rules
<details>
<summary>Click to expand</summary>

- Participants can either participate individually or in team
- Participants may be involved in **only one team** per challenge.
- Exceptions may be granted with **prior approval** from the organizers.
- Teams may compete in all three challenges (Isometric and Dynamic) or in either one of the challenges
- Each team must nominate a team leader responsible for ccordination and submission
- Each team may submit one contribution per challenge. In the case of multiple submissions per challenge by the end of said challenge, the latest submission will be evaluated, while the others are discarded.

</details>
